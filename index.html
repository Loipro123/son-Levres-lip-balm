Lèvres Lip Balm

     If feels good to leave a light lip balm on overnight, so you can wake up feeling refreshed, rejuvenated and ready to start the day. We are proud to present our new improved product. It's perfectly designed for stressful days that really need a little extra recovery - so you feel best in the morning.
     We think a night lipgloss is about a balance of calming, restoring ingredients on your body. And its scent will never let you down.

Ingredients:

  • - Sunflower oil
  • - Jojoba oil
  • - Shea butter
  • - Beeswax
  • - Coconut oil
  • - Evening primrose oil
  • - Tocopherol
  • - Lavender oil
  • - Wild orange oil
  • - Roman chamomile oil
  • - CBD hemp oil (150mg)

How to use:

- Apply a thin layer of lip balm on your lips at night before going to bed for about 15 minutes and leave it on your lips until you wake up.

- Only use 1 time/day.

- Preserving in a dry place, avoid high temperatures, sunshine.
